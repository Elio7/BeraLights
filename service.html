Digital Printing

Our digital print gives you faster turnaround and greater efficiency than ever before, and also allows for new services such as variable data printing where you can differntiate or personalize printed materials by changing text or graphics from page to page without slowing down the printing process.

Offset Printing

Compared to other printing methods, our offset printing is best suited for economically producing large to medium volumes of high quality prints in a manner that requires little maintenance.

Our offset printing is most suitable for printing very large jobs with the same artwork (image). Economically; offset has higher setup costs compared to digital printing, but lower printing cost than any digital print.

We would recommend our offset printing when you have mass volume of pages to print. Whether you're an agency that needs booklets for an upcoming tradeshow or a marketing agent needs promotional flyers, Bera Lights is the best choice for efficient offset printing for your materials.

Our dar-es-salaam based printing facility can deliver afforable offset printing that maintains the professional quality you need, from copy to copy. Our high speed offset presses produce millions of pages monthly for clients within the country and some outsiders.
