Frequently Asked Question

You May Ask

Do you design Logos?
Yes we do. Through our consultation you can have different solutions of introducing your brand into the market.
How do I go about getting an estimate from you?
Send your request with all the necessary details (Including Quantity, Size & dimensions, Type/Formats and timeframe) so as our sales team will quickly respond to it and provide you with an accurate quote. Or you may call us for quick response and help.
How long will it take for you to complete my order?
Every job is different. Some jobs can be produced in minutes while some may take several days to complete. Let us know when you need your job completed and we'll let you know if it can be done. We go to great lengths to meet even your most demanding timelines.
What is your minimum quantity of business card?
We print a minimum of one business card bundle, containing 100pcs neatly packaged and ready for use.
What is a mockup and why is it needed?
They are just a proof file that’s sent to a client for your approvals. Your approval on the final proof is the best assurance you have that every aspect of our work and your own is correct, and that everything reads and appears the way you intended. Mistakes can and sometimes do happen. It benefits everyone if errors are caught in the proofing process rather than after the job is completed and delivered.
Do you give discounts?
Yes we do. Depending on the quantity and type of printing we provide discounts.
I'm just starting my business. What do i need?
We advise you to have a registered business name so that you can effectively brand your business without any fear of brand theft. Please contact us for more details.
How can digital marketing help me?
Digital marketing can help you reach more people who are intrested in your product through social media in a cost effective and hassle free way. Please feel free to contact us for further information.
I need designs only. Do you do that?
Yes we do. We have several clients who want brand themselves effectively through social media. We can help you get started with ease.
I need something good on a tight budget. Can you help?
That is our speciality. Please contact us to let us know what you need and how we can help you.
